About Kulmor Group

A hands-on customer support partner for growing ecommerce brands

Kulmor Group was built around a practical idea: growing brands need support that feels reliable, organized, and personal without becoming overly complicated.

Support built around recruiting, training, and day-to-day management

We stay closely involved in how support is delivered. That starts with recruiting carefully, training agents thoughtfully, and staying hands-on in the day-to-day management of customer support operations.

For brands, that means working with a team that cares about communication quality, consistency, and making support easier to manage as your business grows.

Operations

Built across Albania and Tampa, Florida

Kulmor Group operates from Albania and Tampa, Florida. This split model helps us stay flexible with support coverage, collaboration, and communication while serving ecommerce brands that need dependable day-to-day help.
